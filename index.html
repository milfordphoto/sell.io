Common questions

How do I check my order status?

Use the Check order status page with your quote number and email address to get a secure status link.

How long does inspection take?

Your gear is typically evaluated within 1-2 business days after it arrives at Milford Photo. When evaluation is complete, you will receive an email with your final quote.

What is the difference between cash and store credit?

The cash offer is the amount Milford Photo can pay by check or PayPal after inspection. Store credit gives you 10% more and is available right after your final quote is accepted, so you can use it toward a Milford Photo purchase without waiting for a PayPal payment or mailed check.

Is shipping free?

Qualifying quotes receive a prepaid inbound shipping label by email. The quote and shipping label are valid for 7 days.

What should I include with my gear?

Send the gear, battery, charger, caps, hoods, straps, cables, and other standard original manufacturer accessories when applicable. Missing original accessories may change the final quote.

How should I pack my gear?

Pack each item securely with padding so it cannot move inside the box. If you mail your gear, print the quote page and place it inside the box so Milford Photo can match the shipment to your quote.

Can I bring my gear to the store?

Yes. Choose the store dropoff option and bring the gear to Milford Photo. Staff can use the same quote to check in and evaluate the items at the counter.

Can the final quote change?

Yes. Milford Photo verifies condition, exact model, working condition, and included original manufacturer accessories when your gear arrives. If the condition is different from quoted or original accessories are missing, you will receive a new quote that you can either accept or decline and have the gear returned to you.

How do I get paid?

After Milford Photo evaluates your gear, you will receive a final quote by email. Once you accept it, you can choose how you would like to be paid and enter your payment details.

How long does it take to get paid?

Store credit is available right after your final quote is accepted. PayPal payments are issued within 1-2 business days and usually appear in your PayPal account once PayPal marks the payment completed. PayPal may review or hold some payments depending on the account. If you choose check, Milford Photo mails it within 1-2 business days.

What if I cannot find my exact item?

Choose the closest match only if it is the same model. If the item is not listed, select the manual review or other option and Milford Photo can review it directly.
