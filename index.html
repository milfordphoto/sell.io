How it works

A simple quote now, a careful inspection later.

1

Get an instant quote

Choose the item and condition. The price assumes standard original manufacturer accessories.

2

Send it or bring it in

Ship qualifying quotes with a prepaid label, or bring the gear to Milford Photo.

3

Inspection confirms it

Staff verify each item and email a final line-item quote if anything changes.

How long does inspection take?

Most quotes are reviewed within 1-3 business days after the gear arrives or is dropped off.

Can the final quote change?

Yes. Milford Photo verifies condition, exact model, and included original manufacturer accessories before payment.

When do I choose payout?

You choose check, PayPal, or store credit after the final quote is accepted.

Condition guide

Choose the closest condition

Pick the option that best matches the gear today. Milford Photo will confirm the final condition during inspection.

Like New

Very clean with little to no visible wear. Fully working with clean glass, sensor, and controls.

Excellent

Light normal use. Minor cosmetic marks are okay as long as everything works properly.

Good

Noticeable wear from regular use, but fully usable with no major functional problems.

Well Used

Heavy wear, rough cosmetics, or missing non-critical parts, but still usable.

Heavily Used

Damaged, incomplete, uncertain operation, or needs staff review before a final offer.
